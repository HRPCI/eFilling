Fees paid to the Government of India (via the GST portal, NSDL/UTIITSL, MCA, or Passport Seva Kendra) are governed by the respective government's own refund policy and are generally non-refundable. eFiling Portal Solutions has no control over government fee refunds.

2. Our Service Fee — Refund Conditions

Our professional service fee may be refunded in the following circumstances:

  • Full Refund: If we are unable to initiate your application within the committed timeframe due to a fault on our side, you are eligible for a full refund of the service fee.
  • Partial Refund (50%): If you cancel the service before we begin processing your application (within 24 hours of payment), a 50% refund will be issued after deducting processing charges.
  • No Refund: Once your application has been processed, documents reviewed, and filed with the relevant authority, the service fee is non-refundable — regardless of the outcome of the application.

3. Application Rejection

If your application is rejected by the relevant government authority for any reason — including incomplete documents, verification issues, or eligibility criteria — the service fee is non-refundable. We will, however, assist you in re-applying at a discounted service fee.

4. Refund Process

Approved refunds will be processed within 7–10 working days via the original payment method. To request a refund, email contact@e-filling.in with your order ID and reason for refund.

5. Disputes

Refund disputes must be raised within 30 days of the original transaction. We will respond within 5 working days.
